****Guests****
Our featured guests are WNBA Champion & All-Star MVP Nikki Teasely. Coaching alongisde her are our very own Norman coaches, 6A State Champion and Coach of the Year Mke Neal from Norman High, and 6A State Runnerup Rory Hamilton from Norman North.
